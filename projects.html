Projects

Details about the various scientific projects supported by BINA.

Asteroseismology Projects:

Asteroseismology Study of oscillations in stars to understand their internal structure and evolution.

ORBIT Projects:

ORBIT Observations of red giants and binary interactions through telescopes.

Nucleosynthesis Projects:

Nucleosynthesis Research into the formation of elements in stars.

Multi-wavelength Study of Massive Stars Projects:

Multi-wavelength Study of Massive Stars Observing massive stars across various spectra to study their properties.

Instrumentation Projects:

Instrumentation Projects focused on the development and improvement of astronomical instruments.

Reverberation Mapping Projects:

Reverberation Mapping Study of light echoes to understand the structure around supermassive black holes.
